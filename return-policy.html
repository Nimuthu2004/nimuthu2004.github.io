Return and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 20 March 2026

This Return and Refund Policy applies to LankaBus and explains how refunds, reversals, and transaction issues are handled for wallet top-ups and ticket purchases.

1. Service Type

LankaBus provides digital services including bus ticket booking and wallet recharge. No physical goods are sold or delivered.

2. Wallet Top-Ups

  1. Wallet top-ups are processed by third-party payment providers.
  2. Successfully credited top-ups are generally non-refundable.
  3. If money is debited but not credited to your LankaBus wallet, you may request a transaction review.

3. Ticket Bookings

  1. Ticket payments are charged at the time of booking.
  2. Cancellation and refund eligibility depends on booking status, cancellation time, operator rules, and whether the ticket has already been used/validated.
  3. If approved, the refund may be credited to your LankaBus wallet or to the original payment method when supported.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

  1. Incorrect booking details entered by the user after successful payment.
  2. Change of mind after a successful wallet top-up.
  3. Tickets already used, scanned, or validated.
  4. Policy violations, abuse, or suspected fraudulent activity.

5. Failed or Duplicate Payments

  1. If a payment fails but funds are debited, contact us with proof of payment and reference details.
  2. Duplicate debits are reviewed with the payment gateway and relevant financial providers.
  3. Approved reversals/refunds may take several business days based on banking and gateway processing timelines.

6. How to Request a Refund Review

Please include the following information in your request:

  1. Full name
  2. Registered phone number or email
  3. Transaction or booking reference ID
  4. Date and time of payment
  5. Issue description with screenshot/proof (if available)

7. Processing Time

  1. Initial review is usually completed within 3-5 business days.
  2. Final settlement time depends on external payment and banking systems.

8. Chargebacks and Disputes

We recommend contacting LankaBus support before initiating a chargeback. Unresolved disputes may be handled according to payment provider procedures and applicable regulations.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
